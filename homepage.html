Your committment to integrity and security


Code of Ethics

  • Integrity and Honesty: Always be truthful and transparent about security practices, vulnerabilities, and incidents.

  • Accountability: Take personal responsiblity for your actions and mistakes that affect data and system security.

  • Confidentiality and Respect for Privacy: Protect your sensitive information at all times, ensuring that your personal data and institutional records remain secure.

  • Compliance with Laws and institutional Policies: Ensure you adhere to all applicable legal, regulatory, and international standards related to cyber security.

  • Professionalism and Respect: Be sure to conduct oneself with respect and fairness in all interactions, fostering a secure and inclusive academic environment.

  • Transparency and Open Communication: Make sure you encourage clear communication about security risks and incident reporting, balancing openness with Confidentiality.

  • Ethical Use of Technology: Always make sure you are using institutional and digital resources responsibly, by adhering that technology is leveraged in ways that promote safety and trust

  • Continuous Learning and Improvement: Make sure you commit to ongoing education in cyber security to adapt to emerging threats and evolving ethical challenges.

  • Avoidance of Conflicts and Interests: Be sure that you effectively identify and manage any conflicts that might compromise security or ethical standards.

  • Collaborate Community Engagement: Always ensure that you work together with your peers, faculty, and staff to create a culture of shared responsiblity for security.

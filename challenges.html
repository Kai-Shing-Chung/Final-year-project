Beginner-level User Awareness

Scenario: Urgent University Account Alert

Subject: Urgent - Verify Your University Account Now!


Dear Student,

We have detected unusual activity on your university account that you hold with us at Birmingham City University. To secure your account, please go to the link attached to verify your identity and update your password immediately.


http://birmingham-city-university-login.com


Important: You must complete this verification within 24 hours to avoid your account being suspended until further notice.


Kind Regards,

BCU IT Department


Question: Review the email and identify if this is real or fake.

Activity: The Password Strength Challenge

Imagine you have just recieved an email from your university's IT department asking you to evaluate the strength of your current student account password.


Activity Instructions:

1) Evaluate your current password against the following criteria

  • Length: Is your password at least 12 characters long?

  • Complexity: Does your password include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters?

  • Uniqueness: Is your password unique; not easily guessable such as your name or birthdate?

  • Randomness: Is your password free from predictable patterns (like "1234" or "abcd")?


    • 2) Reflection

    From the above criteria, how would you rate your current password?

  • Weak: It is short, lacks variety, or it is easily guessable


  • Moderate: It meets some criteria but can be improved


  • Strong: It meets all criteria

    Intermediate-level User Awareness

    Scenario: The Password Upgrade Challenge

    Imagine you are working at a government agency, and the IT department mandatory asks you to update your current password. As you recall your password, you already believe you are following best practices


    Activity Instructions:

    1) Self-Evaluation: Think of the password you are currently using, and use an onlne password strength tool such as https://howsecureismypassword.net/ to test your password strength

    2) From the feedback of using the online password strength tool, create a new password and test again

    Question: What did you learn from this exercise? No answer needed, just click submit



    Scenario: The Suspicious Invoice Attachment

    While you are studying at Birmingham City University, you suddenly receive an email that appears genuine from PayPal, with the subject line "Your invoice is attached: Invoice.exe - click to download". However, the link with the attachment is from invoicing-payment@paypal.com instead of service@paypal.co.uk

    Activity: Review both links and identify how the email might be a phishing attempt or contain Malware.





    Scenario: Downloading A Free Game

    While you are browsing on the internet, you visit a website for the first time where you can download any free game you want. However, you are skeptic if the file you just downloaded contains malware or a virus

    Question: Name an online tool you can use to check if the downloaded file is safe to execute or must be removed immediately

    Question: Which network connection would be ideal?

    Scenario: Cyber Incident at an Industry Placement

However, in the afternoon, other departments report they cannot access critical files on the server. It is reported that the incident appears to be a ransomware attack.

    Question: What plan is best suited to handle the above situation?





    Scenario: Evil Twin at a business conference

    You are on an industry placement at KPMG that is part of your degree at Birmingham City University. While you are at a meeting, you want to connect to their free Wi-Fi. However, there are two network connections. One is called "KPMG Wi-Fi" and the other is "KPMG-Free-Wi-FI". As the SSIDs are similar, you are skeptic about which one is legitimate

    Question: What is the non-technical name for two network connections that have the same name?
